Founder & Chairman

Maruti Somanatti

With a vision rooted in integrity, Maruti Somanatti established Khushi Tours & Travels to redefine what service means in Hubballi. His leadership style is quiet, firm, and relentlessly focused on the client's peace of mind.

His philosophy is simple: no compromise. For over two decades, he has been the guardian of our reputation, ensuring every journey meets a standard that goes beyond mere transportation.

Managing Director

Bharat M. Somanatti

Carrying the torch of legacy, Bharat Maruti Somanatti integrates modern logistics precision with traditional values of hospitality. He has pioneered the digitization of our fleet operations, ensuring efficiency without losing the personal touch.

Under his direction, the brand has evolved into a comprehensive mobility partner for Karnataka’s most prestigious corporations, blending innovation with tradition.
